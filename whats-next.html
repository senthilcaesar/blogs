5 Articles to figure out what's next

A curated selection of articles to help you navigate your career, find your passion, and decide on your next big move.

How to Do What You Love by Paul Graham

A deep exploration of how to find work that you are genuinely passionate about and the common pitfalls along the way.

Hell Yeah or No by Derek Sivers

A simple but powerful framework for decision-making: if you're not saying "Hell Yeah!" about something, say no.

How to Pick a Career (That Actually Fits You) by Wait But Why

An exhaustive, visual guide to deconstructing your "Yearning Octopus" and building a career path that aligns with your true self.

Make Your Work Your Calling by The Atlantic

Exploring the shift from seeing work as a job or career to viewing it as a meaningful calling.

The Pmarca Guide to Career Planning by Marc Andreessen

Practical and contrarian advice on how to plan your career in a rapidly changing world.
